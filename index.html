Is sitting up straight the best way to sit?
June 19, 2021




You have probably been told many times that sitting up straight is good and you should do it... but is that actually true?


Well a study in 2006 says that sitting up straight is a bad position for your back to be in. The study said that sitting at 135° is the best for your back.


Another website states that sitting up straight will cause back problems later in life.


In conclusion, sitting at a 135° is probably better for your back and that is how you should sit.
